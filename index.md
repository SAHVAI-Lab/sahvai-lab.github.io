## About

SAHVAI (Subarachnoid Hemorrhage Volumetric AI) is an artificial intelligence system for automated volumetric measurement of subarachnoid hemorrhage on noncontrast head CT scans. 

Developed at Mayo Clinic, SAHVAI enables:
- **Automated SAH volume calculation** from CT images
- **3D volumetric visualization** (SAHVAI-3D)
- **Temporal tracking** across serial scans (SAHVAI-4D)
- **Outcome prediction** via the eSAH score
